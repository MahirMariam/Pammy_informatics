CRISTIANO RONALDO'S CAREER

Major clubs and achievements throughout his legendary football career.

Sporting CP

Cristiano Ronaldo began his professional career at Sporting CP in Portugal. His outstanding performances quickly attracted the attention of Europe's biggest clubs.

Manchester United

In 2003 Ronaldo joined Manchester United, winning three Premier League titles, the UEFA Champions League and his first Ballon d'Or.

Real Madrid

Real Madrid became the most successful period of Ronaldo's career. He won four Champions League trophies and became the club's all-time leading scorer.

Juventus

Ronaldo joined Juventus in 2018 and continued his incredible goal-scoring form, winning two Serie A titles and several individual awards.

Portugal National Team

As captain of Portugal, Ronaldo led his country to victory at UEFA Euro 2016 and the UEFA Nations League, becoming the highest international goalscorer in football history.

Al Nassr

Since joining Al Nassr in Saudi Arabia, Ronaldo has continued breaking records while inspiring football fans around the world with his dedication and professionalism.
